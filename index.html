Research

In 2017 I had the amazing opportunity to work on a research team at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County in conjunction with The University of Maryland, Medial Center. Our team – Johnlemuel Casilag, James Della-Giustina, Elizabeth Gregorio, and me Aniebiet Jacob – implemented an MPI algorithm in our code that parallelized a fast image reconstruction system. This sytem was (and still is) being used for chemotherapy treatments. Our algorithm was written in a combination of C++ and C and utilized Matlab for imaging purposes, and was fully parallelized using MPI on the HPCF cluster maya. We conducted our research in the summer of 2017 at the UMBC REU Site: Interdisciplinary Program in High Performance Computing, with guidance and assistance from our faculty mentor Matthias K. Gobbert and our graduate assistant Carlos Barajas. Check out our publish paper for our research here.
